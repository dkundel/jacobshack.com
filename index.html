What is jacobsHack! ?

jacobsHack! is a student-run hackathon at the international Jacobs University Bremen in Germany. This is what awaits you:

24 hours of hacking!

You have this project you always wanted to code but you never found the time for? Or a functionality you wanted to add to an existing project? In these 24 hours you can work on whatever you feel like. The only condition is:You will only be judged on what you wrote within these 24 hours

+100 fellow hackers

You have the opportunity to meet fellow programmers from all over the world, exchange your knowledge or even find your future business partner with whom you will found your first start-up.

+2000€ in prizes

With your project, you have the chance to win one of our in total more than 2000€ worth of prizes and get eternal fame by being the winner of the first major jacobsHack! event.

Recruiting opportunities

Get in touch with the recruiters of some of our world top employers, exchange contact information or even directly interview with some of them.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does jacobsHack! take place?

jacobsHack! will take place on the campus of Jacobs University Bremen, Germany

I will have already graduated from university. Can I participate?

No, jacobsHack! is meant for university and high school students. You have to be enrolled in an educational facility to be able to participate.

I am still in high school. Can I participate?

Yes! jacobsHack! is open to university and high school students. The most important thing is that you bring passion and have fun!

If you are still under 18, your parents will have to sign a form of consent for us.

How much does registration cost?

Nothing.

Will travel expenses be reimbursed?

Travel expenses for a 2nd class public transport ticket up to 50€ will be reimbursed.

What do I need to bring?

Enthusiasm. Basic toiletries and a sleeping bag in case you are planning to sleep might be useful as well!

Food will be provided during the event. Please make sure to mention special dietary requirements in your registration.

When does registration start?

Soon! If you want to be notified once registration starts please leave your email and we will notify you once we start.

I want to support the event!

Wonderful just send us an email to get more information. Email:  sponsor@jacobshack.com

I have another question!

We got you covered. Email us at team@jacobshack.com
