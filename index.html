AboutjacobsHack!

"I Hack, Therefore I Am!". Under this slogan the foundation for jacobsHack! was laid when on Feb 1, 2014 the students of Jacobs University met to code for 19 hours on whatever project they considered interesting. On October 4th, 2014 we then took the next step and opened jacobsHack! for external students. In total 85 students from 30 nations participated at jacobsHack! Fall creating one of the most culturally diverse hackathons in Europe.

This year we are returning aiming to create one of the greatest and the most diverse hackathon in Europe. Bigger and more exiting then before while sticking to our initial motto:

I Hack, Therefore I Am!

What is jacobsHack!?

24h of hacking

You have this project you always wanted to code but you never found the time for? Now you have the chance to dedicate 24 hours on this project. The only condition is:  You will only be judged on what you wrote within these 24 hours

Meet fellow hackers!

You have the opportunity to meet fellow programmers from all over the world, exchange your knowledge or even find your future business partner with whom you will found your first start-up.

Prizes and Freebies

Thanks to our sponsors you will have the chance to win a variety of awesome prizes and even if you don't win we can promise you you won't leave empty handed!

Career Opportunities

Get the opportunity to meet some of the engineers and recruiters of our sponsors, make contacts and get the chance to potentially interview with some of them.

Where Does jacobsHack! Take Place?

jacobsHack! takes place on the campus of the internation university  Jacobs University Bremen  in Bremen, Germany. The campus is home of over 600 Bachelor students and in total over 1200 students representing 114 nations creating a truely diverse experience that we will try to deliver to the participants of jacobsHack!
